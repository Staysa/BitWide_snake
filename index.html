FAQ

How long after we sign on does outreach start?

Most clients launch within 7-10 business days after we align on your ICP (Ideal Customer Profile), messaging, and data sources. Our team handles setup, copy, and integration fast.

What if my ICP isn’t clear yet?

No problem — we’ll help you define it. We analyze your current customers, markets, and product fit to build a verified prospect list before any outreach begins.

Can you work with our CRM?

Yes. We integrate easily with HubSpot, Salesforce, Pipedrive, and most CRMs via CSV or API. You’ll get full transparency — every contact and message is shareable or exportable. Don't have a CRM? No problem there either, we have one you can use as well.

How do you charge?

Pricing is based on the channels you choose and the volume of outreach desired. Our pricing above is based on the average client need which is about 100 Prospects targeted each week within each channel.

What’s your minimum commitment?

Our minimum commitment is 6 months, and then moves to a month to month after the initial term. It's rare for us to see our customers cancel after six months.
